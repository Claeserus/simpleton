Our Mission

Complexity is the enemy of progress. We exist to make the "hard parts" of technology accessible to everyone.

The Simpleton Philosophy

Most tutorials assume you already know 90% of the jargon. Simpleton was built from a place of frustration—the belief that if you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it well enough.

We focus on projects over theory. Logic over rote memorization. And most importantly, we believe that anyone—regardless of their background—can learn to build for the web.
