Translating scary tech words into plain English.

The Jargon Trap

Developers love big words like "Encapsulation," "Asynchronous," and "Abstraction." These words sound intimidating, but the concepts behind them are usually things you already understand in real life.

Example: Abstraction

When you drive a car, you turn the key and the engine starts. You don't need to know how the spark plugs work. That is abstraction. You use a simple interface to control a complex machine.
